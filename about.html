    About

    Front-End Developer | UI/Web Designer | Video Producer

    Hey there,
    Welcome to my portfolio website! Let me take a minute to introduce myself.

    I started my design journey in 2013. I joined a startup called VV Look. We were creating customizable accessories for glasses which seemed like a very cool idea because It was meant to change how the world thinks about glasses, woohooo! We worked a lot and finally launched our first Kickstarter campaign. Unfortunately, we had less than 20 bakers for first several days and our campaign (and product) failed. I learned my lesson but my love for design never left me since then. I had a chance to join The Walt Disney company and that's when a lot of interesting things started to happen. Tons of work, tight deadlines I've been living in San Francisco start up world for more than two years and I love it here! I was honored to design websites and apps for the smartest people in many different fields like e-commerce, TV, computational biology, business and law and others. Full corporate identity visual with various versions of the logo. The creative combination of letters of the company name: Bespoke Creative LTD. (B+C+L) gave the following effects work. We used soft nude shades colors which added prestige. Website done in HTML5 + CSS3 responsive version.
