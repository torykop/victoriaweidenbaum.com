The goal of the national campaign was to support the new programming block. We were working with an agency in order to determine what kind of content viewers would expect to see on a Disney Channel Russia ad banner. We used familiar characters and lots of pixie dust to show how magical this time slot is. We created city formats and transport banners for more than 20 cities in Russia.
