I started my design journey in 2013 with joining a startup called VV Look. We were creating customizable accessories for glasses which seemed like a very cool idea because It was meant to change how the world thinks about glasses, woohooo! We worked a lot and finally launched our first Kickstarter campaign. Unfortunately, we had less than 20 bakers for first several days and our campaign (and product) failed. I learned my lesson but my love for design never left me since then.
    I've seen a lot since than. I've been living in San Francisco start up world for more than two years and I love it here! I was happy to work with the smartest people designing products in many different fields like e-commerce, TV, computational biology, business and law and others.I am passionate about understanding people and designing engaging experiences for them.
    My favorite part is distilling complicated problems into simple, minimal solutions and iterating fast in order to find out which one actually works.
    I am looking forward to joining a design dream team that cares about users, respects each other's diverse perspective and creates value for people.
